Services

Our services are available in a package or a la carte to offer maximum flexibility. Please note that we are ready and willing to tweak our offerings to meet individual student needs. We started The Essay Advisory because we love working with students, so we want to make sure the process is as streamlined and tailored as possible. Please reach out to us here with questions or to request further information. We look forward to hearing from you!

Full Developmental Package

The Developmental Package is a comprehensive three step process designed to guide you from initial ideas to a strong, fully polished essay. It includes a (40) minute conversation to get to know you as a person and applicant, followed by two remote edits— a developmental edit to refine and encourage the most compelling ideas in your essay, and a polish edit to ensure clarity and flow.

EMAIL US

Remote Editing:

We offer remote reading, editing, and analysis of personal statements, supplemental materials, and scholarship essays. You will receive detailed line edits and feedback throughout your essay, with a focus on messaging, clarity, structure, and style. We will also provide a broader review that summarizes our feedback, assesses the overall strength of the essay, and outlines clear next steps to guide you through the revision process. Essays are submitted at the link provided and returned with edits within five (5) business days.

Remote Consultations:

Remote consultations are available via phone or Zoom and are tailored to each student’s individual needs. Whether you've already received written feedback and want to talk through revisions together, or you're just getting started and need help brainstorming or outlining, we will meet you wherever you are in the process.
