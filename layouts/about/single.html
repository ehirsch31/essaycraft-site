About

Eric

Eric is a Marine Corps veteran, former collegiate walk-on athlete, accomplished amateur classical musician, and current law student. Before joining the military in 2021, he worked as a college advisor and SAT/ACT tutor for four years. Eric is particularly attuned to how lived experience shapes identity, ambitions, and values. As an essay advisor, he helps students communicate the significance of their experiences through compelling narratives that capture the attention of admissions committees.

Ben

Ben has made a career out of helping both professional and amateur writers reach their full potential. As a literary agent, he works closely with leading researchers, journalists, and thought leaders to develop their book proposals, edit their manuscripts, and place essays in major newspapers and magazines. As a college essay advisor, he uses those same tools to help students create engaging and authentic narratives that they feel proud to have written.

He graduated from Yale University with a degree in philosophy, and he lives in London where you can find him reading in parks around the city.
